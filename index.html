About David

David Kim organizes Ruby Rookies and Product Management Fast-Track in San Francisco.

David previously was the head of business development at Hack Reactor. David interacts extensively with top-tier companies in the Bay Area. David is a life hacker who solves problems and enables people in the community.

David shares his experiences and musings on Medium and on blogger. You can follow him on Twitter: @findinbay or Github: dklounge. Ask him a question on Quora, or join him on a long run with Anyone Can Run San Francisco.
